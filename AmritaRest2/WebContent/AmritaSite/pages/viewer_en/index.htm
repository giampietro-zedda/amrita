All legacy informations are connected by relationships stored in the Amrita database and it's possible to get application knowledge starting from any available information.

So may be useful to start from a program, a map, a jcl, a database column or even from a user abend code.

Informations related to objects and relationships are shown by means of drill-down menus and it's not necessary any previous knowledge.

The first time consuming factor in the maintenance activity has always been the general context understanding, to say to get the list of programs and objects to be mainteined and the area in the source code to be modified.

Very old IBM statistics on legacy systems tell that about 50 percent of total maintenance time is spent in the initial understanding activity.

This type of knowledge is essentially achieved getting informations about objects, relationships between objects and the relationship origin in the source code.

Of course it's necessary to get also dynamically generated relatioships between programs and objects, obtained by Amrita Assesment.

Viewer allows you to get this knowledge just with few mouse clicks with a dramatic reduction of the overall maintenance time.
